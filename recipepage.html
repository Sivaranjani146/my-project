Biryani Recipe

To make a delicious biryani, first cook basmati rice until it’s half done. In another pan, sauté onions, tomatoes, and your favorite spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender. Then, layer the rice and chicken together, sprinkle some saffron milk, and cook it on a low flame for a few minutes. Serve hot with raita or gravy. Enjoy the heavenly aroma and taste!
