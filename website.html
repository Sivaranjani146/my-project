What is Briyani?

Briyani is a famous Indian dish made with fragrant basmati rice, spices, and either chiken, mutton, or vegetables. It's known for its rich aroma and flavorful layers.

It's ofteen served during festivals, weddings and celebrations across India. Each region has its own veersion - like Hyderbadi Briyani, Lucknowi Briyani, and Dindigul Briyani!
